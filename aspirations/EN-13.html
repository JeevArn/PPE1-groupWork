We respect and acknowledge the First Nations Peoples of the lands and waters on which we live and work, their rich cultural heritage and deep connection to Country, and we acknowledge their Elders past and present. We are committed to truth-telling and to engaging with First Nations Peoples to support the protection of their culture and heritage. We strongly advocate social, cultural and political justice and support the Uluru Statement from the Heart.
