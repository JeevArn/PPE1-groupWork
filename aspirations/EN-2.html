About Us

EFHA is an international hub, in which fashion GLAMs (Galleries, Libraries, Archives and Museums) and brands share their digital heritage assets and their experiences and best practices in the field of digitisation, access and valorisation of fashion heritage resources.

Read more

Partners

The European Fashion Heritage Association brings together more than 50 institutions coming from 15 European countries, creating the largest fashion heritage network in the world.
